Our Services

Help announcements

Do you need medicine for a sick fox? Is there no one to shelter the fox cub? In the “Announcements” section, you can leave a request for help or offer your own. "Fox Trail” will help you find the right person quickly.

Map

The map shows all the fox shelters and volunteer organizations that the foundation cooperates with. You will be able to find the center closest to you and contact it directly.

Popularization of information

Your support helps us promote the service on social networks, media and other channels to draw attention to the problems of foxes and attract new supporters.

Stories of rescued foxes

find out about the fates of those who have already been helped. Rescue stories are inspiring and show how important each of your support is.

Useful materials

tips on safe interaction with foxes, information about common diseases and ways to prevent them, recommendations for creating a safe space for foxes on your property — all this can be found in the "Knowledge" section.

Transparent reporting

We publish detailed expense reports so that you can see how your help is making a difference in the lives of foxes.
